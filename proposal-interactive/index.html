About Us

Effectively reaching your target customer is a challenge. And evaluating the success of that engagement can be even more difficult. Managing your point of sale, digital advertising, and customer experience across multiple channels is complicated and offers little insight. Shouldn’t it be easier?

We build innovative interactive experiences that put your brand at your customer’s fingertips. Utilizing intuitive technology and leveraging a cloud-based content management system, our solution dynamically delivers your message and seamlessly incites interest, transforming audience members into customers.

Features

  • Right Place, Right Time

    Present your audience with relevant, accurate information when it is both desired and needed through an engaging and compelling medium.

  • Interactive

    Enjoy a media-rich touch screen user experience that increases the value of advertising beyond that of print media and static digital solutions.

  • Point of Sale

    Through the integrated credit card reader supported by a thermal printer, customers can fulfill your advertisements call to action.

  • Dynamic

    Quickly edit or update your content - our secure cloud-based platform immediately sends data across the network.

  • Analytics

    We collect each impression and interaction as well as audience demographics such as gender and age range.

  • Management Dashboard

    Easily review content, analytics, and sales reports online through our user-friendly dashboard.

  • Fully Customizable

    From content to functionality and form factors to user interface design, our solution is fully customizable to fit your advertising needs and brand strategy.

  • Support

    We provide 24/7 on-call full-service software and hardware maintenance as well as network health monitoring for the life of the solution.

Proposal Details

Development & Deployment

  • User Experience & Advertising Design

    City Corridor will work with you to design a unique and mutually agreeable experience, from organization of content within the user interface to device form factors. Further, our in-house graphic design team will work with you and your industry partners to acquire and develop advertising content or sponsorship materials.

  • Content Management

    With our solution, you content not only becomes dynamic but also revenue generating - we put you in control so you can maximize reach and value. Through our cloud-based platform and online management dashboard, we can actively edit content and iterate over functionality to ensure users have the best possible experience.

  • Interaction and Impression Analytics

    Through the integrated camera and network analytic software, we collect audience impression and demographic data, such as gender and age, plus each interaction made through the touch screen. Evaluation of these analytics informs you of actual advertising impressions and helps to inform business development decisions.

  • Network Size

    Determining the correct network size and host locations are key to providing (a) the optimal user experience, (b) an ideal marketing solution for advertisers, and (c) important demographic and usage data required to prove return on investment. Our experience has demonstrated that hotels, visitors centers, popular attractions, malls, and other high-traffic areas are the best locations for placement. City Corridor recommends an initial network size of 15-20 units.

  • Marketing & Sales

    City Corridor will work with you to establish advertising sales responsibilities, advertising and sponsorship rates, and a media kit to assist in sales efforts. Upon contract acceptance, we will develop a calendar for a cooperative pre-launch marketing and sales efforts including a Launch Event to offer special rates to advertisers. The Launch Event will kick off a presale period to enlist advertisers and vendors prior to deployment of the network.

  • Financial Return on Investment

    We have a proven track record with our partners. Our solution generates a positive financial return by generating transactional, advertising and sponsorship revenues while enhancing your overall customer experience.
